# Duplicate Payment Prevention Policy

The organization must not issue two separate payments to the same vendor for the same amount within a 5 business-day window unless each payment is supported by a distinct, uniquely numbered invoice and purchase order.

Finance staff processing an invoice must check the prior 30 days of vendor payment history before approval. Any payment that matches an existing payment to the same vendor within 1% of the amount and within the 5-day window must be held and escalated to the Finance Controller for manual verification before release.

Automated or agent-assisted review systems must flag same-vendor, same-amount payment pairs occurring within the window as a potential duplicate payment, citing this policy, regardless of whether the underlying invoices appear superficially distinct.
