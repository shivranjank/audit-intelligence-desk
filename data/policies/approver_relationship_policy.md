# Approver-Vendor Relationship Policy

Segregation of duties requires that the individual approving a payment to a vendor must not also be the organization's designated relationship owner or primary point of contact for that same vendor, unless a documented dual-control exception has been granted in advance by the Finance Controller.

Any transaction where the approver is identified as the vendor's primary contact, relationship owner, or otherwise has a direct working relationship with the vendor beyond standard procurement interaction must be flagged under this policy and routed to human review, since it represents a segregation-of-duties gap rather than a confirmed violation on its own.
