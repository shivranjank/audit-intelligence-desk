# Expense Approval Threshold Policy

Any single payment of $10,000 or more requires secondary approval from a department head in addition to the standard approver.

Payments deliberately kept just below the $10,000 secondary-approval threshold — for example, a single engagement invoiced or split so that no individual payment reaches the threshold — are considered a violation of this policy regardless of whether each individual payment was otherwise properly documented. This pattern is referred to internally as structuring.

Reviewers must treat any payment between $9,500 and $9,999.99 as requiring additional scrutiny: confirm whether the payment is part of a larger engagement that should have been aggregated and routed through secondary approval.
