# Off-Hours Approval Policy

Standard business hours for payment approval are Monday through Friday, 08:00 to 18:00 local time. Approvals recorded outside this window, or on a weekend, must include a documented business justification (e.g., urgent vendor deadline, month-end close) attached to the transaction record.

An approval timestamped outside standard business hours with no attached justification must be treated as elevated risk and flagged for supervisory review under this policy, even if the amount and vendor are otherwise unremarkable. This control exists because off-hours approvals are more likely to bypass the informal oversight present during normal working hours.
