# Vendor Banking Detail Verification Policy

Any change to a vendor's registered banking details must be independently verified by contacting the vendor through a previously known and validated communication channel — never through contact information supplied alongside the change request itself.

A payment must not be released on the same business day that a vendor's banking details were changed. A minimum 2 business-day cooling-off period is required between a banking detail change and the first payment made under the new details, to allow verification to complete.

Any transaction description indicating that payment was routed to details updated on or near the invoice date, without documented independent verification, must be flagged under this policy and routed to the Accounts Payable Fraud Review queue rather than paid automatically.
